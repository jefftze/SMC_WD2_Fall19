German Shepherds Ready for a Forever Home

Adoption fees vary with each dog and typically range from $150-$500. They are set, taking into consideration the dog’s age, training level, temperament, if they’re newly arrived, medical condition(s), if any, and other factors. Adoptions fees are used not only to rescue and place the dog you are personally adopting, but to aid in the future rescue and rehabilitation of other deserving dogs. Adoption fees are necessary; without fees, our rescue efforts would quickly come to an end.
