Westside German Shepherd Rescue

We welcome you to the Westside German Shepherd family!


Westside German Shepherd Rescue of Los Angeles is a non profit 501(c)3, no kill rescue committed to saving all types of German Shepherd Dogs from high kill shelters and adopting them to loving, qualified homes.

Westside German Shepherd Rescue Trainers

Charles Perry

Charles Perry is one of the most gifted trainers we have watched work his magic over and over. He knows what the dogs are thinking and guides them and teaches them what is expected. He creates a partnership with their owners or adopters, we hear how they love learning and working with Charles as much as the dogs do!

Contact Charles at cttobedience@gmail.com

Emily Watson

Emily will be holding our Saturday group training classes at the rescue on Hill Street. First class is free for our adopters. You can then sign up for a package of 4 group lessons for $120, Saturdays from 10am-11am. She also does private training, obedience, agility training, sport obedience, trick/movie training.

Contact Emily at emily@mydogismyrock.com

Eran Shine

Eran Shine works with dogs and helping adopters find the right match. He is an incredible problem solver, if you have any settling in problems, he is a wonderful asset. Eran works through issues newly arriving dog might have that need to be addressed before they go to foster homes or permanent homes!

Contact Eran at info@manofdogs.com
